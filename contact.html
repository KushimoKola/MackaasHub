Frequently Ask Questions

What does the Company do

MARKAAS HUB is a marketplace that provides location based job matching solutions for homes and offices by connecting qualified service experts such as plumbers, electricians, carpenters, painters, bricklayers, beauticians, tailors, inverter/solar panel installers, generator repairers and more.

What cities do you serve??

Currently, we serve the city of Lagos and we are working tirelessly to cover all cities and towns in Nigeria. But, just in case you need our services outside Lagos, we can leverage on our network to ensure your job is done. Talk to us!
